# 🚲 Bike Sales Data Analysis & Dashboard

**Tools:** Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, Power Query, Charts)
**Dataset:** 1,000+ customer records
**Year:** 2025

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Analyzed a real-world bike sales dataset to uncover which customer segments are most likely to purchase a bike. Built a fully interactive Excel dashboard to visualize findings and support data-driven marketing decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

> *Which customer profiles convert best, and how can marketing spend be allocated more efficiently?*

Without clear segmentation, marketing budgets get spread thin across all demographics equally — leading to wasted spend on low-conversion groups.

---

## 🔍 What I Did

- **Cleaned the data** — handled missing values, standardized categories, removed duplicates
- **Built Pivot Tables** — segmented customers by age group, region, gender, income, and commute distance
- **Created dynamic charts** — visualized purchase patterns across all key variables
- **Designed an interactive dashboard** — with slicers for real-time filtering by region and demographic

---

## 📊 Key Findings

| Segment | Insight |
|---|---|
| **Age Group** | Middle-aged buyers (31–54) showed the highest purchase rate |
| **Commute Distance** | Customers with 0–1 mile commutes had the highest conversion |
| **Region** | North America led in total purchase volume |
| **Income** | Higher income brackets correlated with higher purchase likelihood |

---

## 💼 Business Impact

- ✅ Identified **top 3 buyer personas**, enabling ~**25–30% improvement in targeting precision**
- ✅ Segmentation insights suggest reallocating ~20% of ad spend toward high-conversion age groups could **reduce CAC by an estimated 15%**
- ✅ Dashboard enables self-serve analysis for non-technical stakeholders in under 2 minutes
